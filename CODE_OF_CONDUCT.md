# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus feedback on ideas and implementation, not people.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent ban from participation.
